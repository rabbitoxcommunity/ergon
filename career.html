Work at Ergon

At Ergon we don’t believe in work-life balance. No, we believe in work-life harmony. Being able to do your best work must enable you to live your best life and vice versa.

Grow with Curiosity

Be guided by curiosity to push boundaries constantly Grow with the process, not just the success of a project.

Team Work

The sum of all our work is always greater than that of any one of us. A collaborative approach is essential.

True Confidence

Confident in what you believe, but not afraid to update your beliefs when presented with valid evidence.

Why>What>How

Why we solve a problem is more important than what the problem is, which is more important than how we solve it.

Our Openings

We're putting together a team of stellar individuals with different ideas and
backgrounds to shape the future of EV technology.
